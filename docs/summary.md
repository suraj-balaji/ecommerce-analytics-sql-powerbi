# Manager Summary – E-Commerce Analytics Project

This dashboard analyzes sales, customer behavior, retention, and product performance using SQL and Power BI.

## Key Findings:

* Revenue reached **13.59M**, driven by a small number of high-performing categories.
* Customer retention is low, with significant drop-off after the first purchase.
* Most customers fall into "At Risk" and "Regular" segments, indicating engagement challenges.
* Cross-selling opportunities exist but are currently underutilized.

## Business Impact:

* Improving retention can significantly increase revenue without acquiring new customers.
* Optimizing product categories can improve profitability and efficiency.
* Implementing cross-sell strategies can increase average order value.

## Recommended Actions:

* Invest in customer retention strategies (email, loyalty programs).
* Focus on top-performing product categories.
* Introduce targeted cross-selling and bundling strategies.

This project demonstrates how data-driven insights can guide strategic decisions in e-commerce.
