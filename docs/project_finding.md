# E-Commerce Analytics – Insights & Recommendations

## 📊 Executive Summary Insights

* Total Revenue reached **13.59M**, with **99K orders** and **96K customers**.
* Revenue shows steady growth from 2017 to 2018 with slight fluctuations in later months.
* Top categories like *beleza_saude* and *relogios_presentes* contribute the majority of revenue.

---

## 🛒 Product & Revenue Insights

* Revenue is highly concentrated in a few categories → **Pareto effect (80/20 rule)**.
* Some high-priced categories generate low revenue → **pricing or demand mismatch**.
* Lower contribution categories indicate **inventory inefficiency**.

### ✅ Recommendations

* Focus marketing on top-performing categories.
* Re-evaluate pricing for low-performing high-price categories.
* Optimize inventory by reducing low-demand products.

---

## 👥 Customer (RFM) Insights

* Majority of customers fall into **"At Risk" and "Regular" segments**.
* **VIP customers contribute high revenue despite low volume**.
* Loyal customers exist but are not growing fast enough.

### ✅ Recommendations

* Launch **retention campaigns for "At Risk" customers**.
* Create **VIP loyalty programs**.
* Upsell Regular → Loyal customers.

---

## 🔁 Cohort (Retention) Insights

* Retention drops sharply after **Month 1**.
* Very few customers return after **Month 3**.
* Indicates weak long-term engagement.

### ✅ Recommendations

* Introduce **email remarketing campaigns**.
* Offer **discounts after first purchase**.
* Build **subscription or repeat purchase incentives**.

---

## 🧺 Market Basket Insights

* Most product combinations have **low frequency (pair_count)**.
* Some pairs show **high lift but low support** → niche combinations.
* Weak bundling patterns across categories.

### ✅ Recommendations

* Use **cross-selling recommendations based on lift**.
* Promote frequently bought pairs in product pages.
* Improve bundling strategy for related categories.

---

## 🎯 Final Business Recommendation

Focus on:

1. **Customer Retention (highest impact)**
2. **Category Optimization**
3. **Cross-selling Strategy**

This will directly improve:
✔ Revenue growth
✔ Customer lifetime value
✔ Repeat purchase rate
